Biography

Dr. Jane Smith completed her medical education at Johns Hopkins University and holds prestigious certifications in Otolaryngology. With over 15 years of clinical and surgical experience, she has established herself as a leader in the field of Ear, Nose, and Throat care.

She specializes in advanced sinus surgery using minimally invasive techniques and has a profound dedication to pediatric ENT care, ensuring the youngest patients receive the gentlest and most effective treatments available.
